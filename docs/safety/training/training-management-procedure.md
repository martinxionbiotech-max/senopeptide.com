# Training Management Procedure

## 1. Training Levels

| Level | Personnel | Training Content | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | All employees | Safety orientation, chemical hygiene, emergency response | Upon hire, annually |
| Technical | Lab/production staff | SOP training, equipment operation, analytical methods | Upon assignment, annual refresher |
| Advanced | Senior scientists | Method development, troubleshooting, validation | As needed |
| Management | Supervisors | QMS, regulatory, deviation investigation | Annually |

## 2. Training Record Requirements

| Element | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Training Form | Title, date, trainer, trainee, content covered |
| Competency Assessment | Written test (≥80%) or practical demonstration |
| SOP Training | Read-and-sign + observed performance |
| Re-training | After SOP revision or >12 months gap |
| Record Retention | Current + 2 years after termination |
