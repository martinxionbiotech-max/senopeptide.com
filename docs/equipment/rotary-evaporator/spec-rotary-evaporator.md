# Rotary Evaporator — Technical Specification

## 1. Technical Parameters

| Parameter | Specification |
|---|---|
| Flask Volume | 1 – 5 L |
| Rotation Speed | 20 – 280 rpm |
| Temperature Range (Bath) | 20 – 100 °C |
| Temperature Accuracy | ±1 °C |
| Vacuum Level | 10 – 100 mbar |
| Condenser | Coil or cold finger, −10 °C |
| Vapor Temperature Sensor | PT100 |

## 2. Typical Settings for Peptide Processing

| Solvent | Bath Temp | Vacuum | Rotation |
|---|---|---|---|
| TFA | 40 °C | 50 – 80 mbar | 150 rpm |
| DCM | 35 °C | 300 – 500 mbar | 120 rpm |
| ACN | 40 °C | 200 – 400 mbar | 120 rpm |
| Diethyl ether | 25 °C | 400 – 600 mbar | 100 rpm |
