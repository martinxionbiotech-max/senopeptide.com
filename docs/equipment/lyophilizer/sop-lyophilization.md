# Lyophilizer — Standard Operating Procedure

## 1. Pre-Operation Checks

- [ ] Verify condenser temperature reaches −80 °C
- [ ] Check vacuum pump oil level and condition
- [ ] Confirm shelf cleanliness
- [ ] Verify temperature probe calibration
- [ ] Check drain valve is closed
- [ ] Review cycle parameters in batch record

## 2. Loading Procedure

1. Pre-cool shelves to 4 °C or specified loading temperature
2. Load trays or vials containing peptide solution
3. Insert temperature probes in representative samples
4. Close chamber door and verify seal integrity

## 3. Freeze-Drying Cycle

| Stage | Temperature | Ramp Rate | Hold Time | Vacuum |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Freezing | −40 °C | 1.0 °C/min | 2 – 4 h | Atmospheric |
| Primary Drying | −10 °C | 0.5 °C/min | 12 – 36 h | 0.05 – 0.1 mbar |
| Secondary Drying | +25 °C | 0.3 °C/min | 4 – 8 h | <0.02 mbar |

## 4. Termination Criteria

- Product temperature within 2 °C of shelf temperature
- Vacuum level stable for ≥1 h
- Pressure rise test (<0.01 mbar/min)
- Residual moisture target: ≤1.0% (by Karl Fischer)

## 5. Unloading Procedure

1. Backfill chamber with dry N₂ to atmospheric pressure
2. Open vent valve slowly
3. Remove product trays
4. Immediately transfer to sealed containers with desiccant
5. Document batch yield and appearance

## 6. Cleaning and Maintenance

| Frequency | Activity |
|---|---|
| After each cycle | Wipe shelves with 70% IPA |
| Weekly | Condenser drain and defrost |
| Monthly | Vacuum pump oil check and filter inspection |
| Quarterly | Temperature probe calibration |
| Annually | Full PM including seal replacement and pump overhaul |
