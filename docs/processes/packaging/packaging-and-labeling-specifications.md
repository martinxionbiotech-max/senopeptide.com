# Packaging and Labeling Specifications

## 1. Primary Packaging

| Container Type | Material | Suitability |
|---|---|---|
| Vial | Amber borosilicate glass (Type I) | Light-sensitive peptides |
| Vial | Clear borosilicate glass (Type I) | Standard peptides |
| Centrifuge tube | Polypropylene (PP) | Short-term storage |
| Cryovial | Polypropylene (PP) | Long-term −20/−80 °C |

## 2. Label Content

| Field | Required | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Product Name | Yes | Semaglutide |
| Lot Number | Yes | 202607-001 |
| Quantity | Yes | 10 mg |
| Purity | Yes | ≥98% |
| Storage Condition | Yes | −20 °C |
| Date of Manufacture | Yes | 2026-07-15 |
| Expiry Date | Conditional | Based on stability |

## 3. Storage Conditions

| Condition | Duration |
|---|---|
| −20 °C (lyophilized, desiccated) | 24 – 36 months |
| −20 °C (in solution) | 3 – 6 months |
| 4 °C (lyophilized, desiccated) | 6 – 12 months |
| Room temperature (lyophilized) | <1 month |
